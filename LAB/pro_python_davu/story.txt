In neon-drenched Neo-Tokyo, teen inventor Anya craved the stars, fueled by whispers of a forgotten past. Amidst her grandfather's dusty attic,
she unearthed plans for a spaceship. 
Driven by grief and a thirst for adventure, Anya spent months scavenging scrapyards and deciphering cryptic notes.
Finally, her ramshackle vessel stood complete. With a pounding heart, she launched, the once-familiar cityscape shrinking below. 
Tears streamed down her face as she pierced the artificial sky, stars igniting for the first time in human memory.
Anya, a lone explorer, chased hope into the vast unknown.
